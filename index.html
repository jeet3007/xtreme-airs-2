About Us

Welcome to Extremeair Pvt. Ltd.

Extreme Air System Pvt. Ltd is committed to manufacturing as well as design and development of Twin Lobe Blower, Air Compressor, Water Cooled Compressor, Water ring Vacuum Pump and Cement Feeding Systems. You can visit out Product Range for more insight into this.

  • Extreme Air System Pvt. Ltd has started its activity with newest vision and with best available contemporary technical knowledge backed by long industry experience and wisdom. Before starting this venture, directors of the company were actively involved in design, development, manufacturing and marketing of the compressors and allied systems.
  • The highly qualified directors are the foundation of the company. Company is determined to provide best quality at affordable price by use of latest engineering & quality assurance procedures and degree holder employees. Optimum use of Computer Aided Design software, 3D modeling, stress & thermal analysis software has made it possible to achieve above objective. In this way, devotion to quality with affordability is ingrained in each and every procedures, from design to after sale service, of the company.
